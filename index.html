Web Fundamentals contains 6 topics
for the Web Development class.

  1. A Bird’s-Eye View of the Web
  2. Web Architecture & Technologies
  3. Web APIs
  4. The Semantic Web & Linked Data
  5. Linked Data Publishing
  6. Re-decentralizing the Web

I’m Ruben Verborgh, researcher and
professor of decentralized Web technology.

  • I’m a researcher at
    • KNoWS, IDLab, Ghent University – imec, Belgium 🇧🇪
    • Oxford Martin School, University of Oxford, UK 🇬🇧
  • I’m a consultant for companies such as
    • Inrupt, USA 🇺🇸
  • I publish blog posts, articles, and books
    about my passion: the Web 🌐

These slides do not only include the truth, they also include my personal opinion.

  • Opinions are marked with the symbol.
    • The Web was invented in 1990.
    • Ted Nelson finds the Web rather limited.
    • The Web is the best invention of the past 30 years.
  • Always know which is which.
    • You are free to reproduce my opinion (with arguments),
    • or state one of your own (with arguments).

The more you participate,
the more you’ll learn.

  • Think along, speak up,
    don’t hesitate to prove me wrong.
    • I’m especially happy to discuss opinions.
  • Without further ado, let’s start
    with an overview of the Web (and the course).

Does the Web pique your curiosity?
Do you want to advance Web research?

  • We are always on the lookout
    for bright master’s thesis students.
    • Pick a Web or Linked Data topic… or make your own!
  • Mail me, tweet to me, or come have a chat.
    • You can inform yourself without any commitment.
  • I wish you the best in your future endeavours,
    on the Web or elsewhere!
